Pointless websites to keep you busy

  1. Want to discover new music?

    Try out Gnoosic!


    Gnoosic uses your top 3 fav. bands to help you find new artists! Even if the new artists are crappy, it's always fun to hear new music.

  2. How about play a lil game?

    Try out Drench!


    Drench is a super simple game, where you simply have to get the entire board to be one color before your moves run out! It's oddly addictive.

  3. Maybe go on an adventure?

    Try out Map Crunch


    Map Crunch drops you in a random place on earth using google maps, letting you explore spaces you never even knew existed! We might not be able to travel as much due to COVID, but that doesn't mean we can't see the world!

  4. Or even vent into a black hole?

    Try outScream into the Void


    Can't afford a counselor? Well Scream into the Void is the website for you! You can type out your problems before sending them into a black hole, never having to worry about them again!
